Kothapatnam Beach

Kothapatnam Beach is located approximately 25 km from Ongole in the Prakasam district of Andhra Pradesh. It lies along the Bay of Bengal, offering a serene coastal experience.he beach is known for its golden sands, clear blue waters, and peaceful ambiance. It is an ideal spot for visitors seeking relaxation away from city life.

Activities & Attractions

Swimming & Sunbathing – Visitors can enjoy the shallow waters and relax on the sandy shore. Picnicking – Families and groups often gather for outings and enjoy the fresh sea breeze. Photography – The scenic landscape makes it a perfect spot for photography, especially during sunrise and sunset. Boating & Fishing – Some local fishermen offer boat rides, and visitors can observe traditional fishing methods.

Festivals & Celebrations

The beach becomes vibrant during festivals such as Sankranti and New Year’s Eve, where people gather for celebrations, kite flying, and cultural events.

Facilities & Accessibility

Food Stalls – Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood and local delicacies from nearby stalls. Parking & Seating Areas – Basic amenities like parking and resting areas are available. Road Connectivity – The beach is easily accessible from Ongole by private vehicles, buses, and autos.

Fisheries and Economic Benefits

The reservoir created by the Mallavaram Dam has created a habitat for aquatic life, making it an essential resource for local fishermen. Fish farming is a vital economic activity in the region, providing additional income for local communities. Moreover, the dam's ability to sustain farming and fisheries significantly contributes to the region's overall economic development.

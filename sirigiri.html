Sri Giri Temple

Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara

A sacred Hindu temple devoted to Lord Vishnu's incarnation, Lord Venkateswara.

Third Most Significant Venkateswara Temple in Andhra Pradesh, after Tirumala Tirupati and Dwaraka Tirumala.

Location: Situated on Srigiri Hill, Seetha Ram Puram, Devuni Cheru, Ongole.

Established: 1997, built under the leadership of Sri Aluru Venkata Ramana Rao with support from his family and friends.

The temple was constructed under the guidance of Sri Aluru Venkata Ramana Rao, the chief promoter of the Neeraja Group of Companies, with support from his brothers and friends.

The consecration (Maha Pratishta) of the temple was completed in 1997, making it a significant pilgrimage site for devotees.

Daily rituals at the temple commence with the 'Prabhodhotsavam' or awakening ceremony at 5 AM, which attracts many devotees.

Famous Events & Festivals of Ongole

Discover the vibrant celebrations of Ongole.

Explore Now

Makara Sankranti

A major harvest festival celebrated with traditional Rangoli, kites, and delicious sweets.

Sankranti Celebrations: Sankranti is marked by traditional events such as bull races and ram fights. The city buzzes with markets, festivities, and cultural events.

Ugadi

The Telugu New Year celebrated with joy, special dishes, and cultural programs.

Ugadi Celebrations: Includes rituals, mango decorations, and dishes like Ugadi Pachadi symbolizing life’s flavors.

Vinayaka Chavithi

People celebrate Lord Ganesha's birthday with grand processions and clay idols.

Vinayaka Chavithi: Idols, rituals, sweets, cultural events and processions. Final day involves immersion and chants.

Kalaralu

Kalaralu is a unique festival in Ongole that reflects rural traditions and heritage.

Kalaralu Festival: Features bull races, folk dances, and mythological drama—showcasing Ongole’s folk legacy.

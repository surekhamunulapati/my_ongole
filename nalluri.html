Nalluri Gardens

Nalluri Garden Resort, often referred to as Nalluri Gardens, is a prominent resort located in Kothapatnam, approximately 15 kilometers from Ongole, Andhra Pradesh. Address: Beach Road, Kothapatnam, Andhra Pradesh 523286. Ideal for Photography – The scenic landscape and well-maintained gardens make it a great spot for photography and videography.

Event Hosting

The resort is renowned for accommodating a variety of events, including weddings, birthday parties, corporate gatherings, and kitty parties. Modern Facilities – Equipped with modern amenities, including a banquet hall, outdoor seating, and ample parking. Serene Ambience – Surrounded by lush greenery, providing a peaceful and picturesque setting for events.

Facilities:

Accommodation: The resort offers 14 air-conditioned rooms for guests. Recreational Amenities: Guests can enjoy an outdoor swimming pool, indoor games, a children's play area, and a lounge. Dining: An on-site restaurant and café provide diverse culinary options. Specific information regarding the resort's inauguration date is not readily available.

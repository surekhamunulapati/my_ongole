Calvary Temple, Ongole

Calvary Temple in Ongole, Andhra Pradesh is a prominent Christian church known for its rapid construction and significant community impact. The temple was remarkably built in just 90 days, showcasing efficient planning and execution. It features modern architectural elements, creating a serene environment for worshippers.

Calvary Temple actively engages with the local community, organizing events like the Calvary Mega Family Fest to address family-related issues through scriptural teachings.

Calvary Temple in Ongole is an extension of the original Calvary Temple founded by Pastor Satish Kumar. The main temple, located in Hyderabad, was established with a vision to accommodate a large congregation and has grown significantly over the years. The Ongole branch continues this mission, providing a place for worship and community gatherings.
